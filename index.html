LifeCaptain – AI Co-Pilot for Personal Growth

LifeCaptain is a futuristic productivity assistant that uses character-driven AI to help users organize tasks, plan missions, and reflect on personal growth. I designed the entire app system including voice interaction flows, mission-based structuring, glowing avatar effects, and a dark futuristic interface. LifeCaptain blends science fiction-inspired UI with calming, motivational UX. All visuals are handcrafted and built for mobile-first usage. Still in development — not yet live.

HookGen Studio – Viral Video Hook Generator

HookGen Studio is a content creation assistant for TikTok/Shorts creators. It helps creators instantly generate high-converting video hook lines and draft full scripts using GPT. I designed and developed the app's UI, brand system, and onboarding flow. It uses motion effects and conversion-first design to keep creators in flow. Currently in internal testing and not yet public.
